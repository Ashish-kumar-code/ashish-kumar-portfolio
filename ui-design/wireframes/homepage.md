# Homepage Wireframe

---

# Goal

The homepage should communicate Ashish Kumar's technical expertise, engineering mindset, and professional credibility within the first 30 seconds of a recruiter's visit.

The homepage is designed to guide visitors through a structured journey:

Introduce → Build Trust → Show Proof → Demonstrate Skills → Encourage Contact

Primary Goals:

- Introduce personal brand
- Highlight flagship projects
- Showcase engineering capabilities
- Increase resume downloads
- Encourage recruiter contact

Success Criteria:

• Recruiters understand profile in under 30 seconds

• Projects receive more attention than resume

• Clear navigation

• Mobile-friendly experience

• Strong visual hierarchy
